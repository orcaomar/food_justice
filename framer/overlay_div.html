Create a platform for all people in the neighborhood who are interested in producing food on their own. In their backyard, on their balconies, in their apartment, wherever they can. And also, we are, at the same time, training and building capacity of those who want to make food production as a small business for themselves. Not only produce food for their own use, but also produce food, and make it accessible to the neighborhood, and make money on that. To make it sustainable, as a sustainable micro farm. [Worker]
